Welcome to The Comfort Stay Men's PG

Welcome to The Comfort Stay Men's PG, a premium accommodation designed for working professionals, IT employees, students, and interns in Saravanampatti, Coimbatore.

Our PG offers clean and spacious rooms, comfortable bedding, high-speed WiFi, CCTV security, kitchen access, washing machine facilities, drinking water, and a peaceful living environment.

Located near CHIL SEZ IT Park, it provides easy access to major IT companies, colleges, restaurants, supermarkets, and public transport.
